Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services, pricing, and procedures.

General Questions

What services does Stopper Tech offer?

+

We offer a comprehensive range of IT services including cyber cafe facilities, computer repair, software installation, government document assistance, student registration services, mobile services, and more. Visit our Services page for a complete list.

What are your operating hours?

+

We are open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We're closed on major holidays.

Do you provide home service for computer repair?

+

Yes, we offer on-site computer repair services for your convenience. Additional charges may apply for home visits. Please contact us to schedule an appointment.

How long does it typically take to repair a computer?

+

Repair time varies depending on the issue. Simple software problems can be fixed within 1-2 hours, while hardware repairs may take 1-3 days. We'll provide an estimated timeline after diagnosing your device.

Cyber Cafe Services

Do I need to book a computer in advance?

+

Booking is not required for regular use, but we recommend calling ahead during peak hours or for extended sessions. Gaming stations can be reserved in advance.

What software is available on your computers?

+

Our computers come with Windows OS, Microsoft Office suite, web browsers, PDF readers, image editing software, and various productivity tools. Gaming PCs have additional gaming software and platforms.

Can I use my own USB drive or external storage?

+

Yes, you can use your own USB drives and external storage devices. We scan all external devices for security before use.

Government Services

What documents do I need for Aadhaar card services?

+

For new Aadhaar registration, you need proof of identity and address documents. For updates, bring your existing Aadhaar card and supporting documents for the changes you want to make.

How long does PAN card application take?

+

PAN card applications typically take 15-20 working days for processing. You'll receive an acknowledgment receipt immediately after application submission.

Do you help with passport applications?

+

Yes, we assist with passport application form filling, document verification, and appointment booking. We also help with passport renewal and other related services.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, UPI payments, debit/credit cards, and digital wallets. For government services, some fees may need to be paid online directly to government portals.

Do you offer any discounts for students?

+

Yes, we offer special student discounts on cyber cafe usage and certain services. Please bring your valid student ID to avail of these discounts.

Is there a warranty on repair services?

+

Yes, we provide a 30-day warranty on all repair services and replaced parts. Software-related fixes come with a 15-day warranty.
