NTSA Services We Offer

Professional assistance with all National Transport and Safety Authority services. Our experienced team ensures your NTSA service requests are handled promptly and efficiently for all your transport needs.

Driving License Application

Apply for your driving license online quickly and easily. Our comprehensive service covers learner's permits, provisional licenses, and full driving license applications with all required documentation.

What You Need:

  1. National ID or Passport copy
  2. Valid email address and phone number
  3. Passport-size photographs
  4. Medical certificate from approved doctor
  5. Driving school certificate (for new applicants)
  6. Previous license (for renewals/upgrades)

Process:

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp with your application details
  2. We verify all required documents
  3. Submit your application to NTSA portal
  4. Schedule driving test (if required)
  5. Receive your driving license via email/collection

Vehicle Registration

Register your vehicle with NTSA quickly and efficiently. Our service covers new vehicle registration, transfer of ownership, and registration renewals with proper documentation.

What You Need:

  1. Vehicle import documents or sales agreement
  2. National ID copy of owner
  3. KRA PIN certificate
  4. Vehicle inspection certificate
  5. Insurance certificate
  6. Previous logbook (for transfers)

Process:

  1. Contact us with your vehicle details and documents
  2. We verify all documentation requirements
  3. Submit registration application to NTSA
  4. Process payment and fees
  5. Receive new logbook and number plates
Register Vehicle Now

PSV License Application

Apply for Public Service Vehicle license for commercial transport operations. Our service covers PSV badges, route licenses, and all commercial vehicle permits required by NTSA.

What You Need:

  1. Valid driving license (minimum 3 years)
  2. National ID copy
  3. Medical certificate
  4. Good conduct certificate
  5. Vehicle logbook and insurance
  6. Route application documents

Process:

  1. Contact us with your PSV application requirements
  2. We review and verify all documents
  3. Submit PSV license application to NTSA
  4. Process route licensing and permits
  5. Receive PSV badge and operating licenses
Apply for PSV License Now

Logbook Services

Comprehensive logbook services including duplicate logbook applications, logbook corrections, and vehicle ownership transfers. We handle all logbook-related services efficiently.

What You Need:

  1. National ID copy of vehicle owner
  2. Police abstract (for lost logbooks)
  3. Vehicle inspection certificate
  4. Current insurance certificate
  5. Transfer documents (for ownership changes)
  6. Correction supporting documents (if applicable)

Process:

  1. Contact us with your logbook service requirements
  2. We assess and verify required documentation
  3. Submit application to NTSA for processing
  4. Track application status and updates
  5. Receive new or corrected logbook
