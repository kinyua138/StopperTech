SHA Registration

Register for Social Health Authority services online quickly and easily. Our straightforward process ensures you get registered for healthcare coverage and receive your SHA certificate promptly via Email and WhatsApp.

What You Need:

  1. National ID or Passport copy
  2. Valid email address
  3. Phone number
  4. Employment details (if employed)
  5. Next of kin information

Process:

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp with your details
  2. We verify your information
  3. Submit your application to SHA
  4. Receive your SHA registration confirmation and certificate

SHA Certificate Retrieval

Retrieve your SHA Certificate online quickly and easily. Our fast and reliable process ensures you receive your Social Health Authority Certificate directly via Email and WhatsApp in minutes.

What You Need:

  1. Your SHA number (if available)
  2. National ID number
  3. Valid email address
  4. Phone number
  5. Date of birth

Process:

  1. Contact us with your SHA details and ID information
  2. We verify your information with SHA
  3. Retrieve your certificate from SHA portal
  4. Send certificate to your email and WhatsApp immediately

SHA Mobile Number Update

Update your mobile number linked to your SHA account easily. Our smooth and efficient service guarantees your updated contact details are processed and your updated SHA Certificate is delivered to your Email and WhatsApp.

What You Need:

  1. Your SHA number
  2. National ID copy
  3. Current mobile number
  4. New mobile number
  5. Valid email address

Process:

  1. Contact us with your SHA details and new mobile number
  2. We verify your identity and current information
  3. Update mobile number on SHA portal
  4. Send confirmation and updated certificate

SHA Beneficiary Management

Manage your SHA beneficiaries including adding dependents, updating beneficiary information, and ensuring your family members are covered under your Social Health Authority account.

What You Need:

  1. Your SHA number
  2. National ID copy
  3. Beneficiary details (ID copies, birth certificates)
  4. Relationship proof documents
  5. Valid email address and phone number

Process:

  1. Contact us with your SHA details and beneficiary information
  2. We verify all documents and relationships
  3. Add or update beneficiaries on SHA portal
  4. Send updated certificate with beneficiary details
