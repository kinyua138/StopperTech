NSSF Registration

Register for National Social Security Fund services online quickly and easily. Our straightforward process ensures you get registered for retirement benefits and receive your NSSF certificate promptly via Email and WhatsApp.

What You Need:

  1. National ID or Passport copy
  2. Valid email address
  3. Phone number
  4. Employment details and employer information
  5. Bank account details
  6. Next of kin information

Process:

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp with your details
  2. We verify your information and employment status
  3. Submit your application to NSSF
  4. Receive your NSSF registration confirmation and certificate

NSSF Certificate Retrieval

Retrieve your NSSF Certificate online quickly and easily. Our fast and reliable process ensures you receive your National Social Security Fund Certificate directly via Email and WhatsApp in minutes.

What You Need:

  1. Your NSSF number (if available)
  2. National ID number
  3. Valid email address
  4. Phone number
  5. Date of birth
  6. Employment information

Process:

  1. Contact us with your NSSF details and ID information
  2. We verify your information with NSSF
  3. Retrieve your certificate from NSSF portal
  4. Send certificate to your email and WhatsApp immediately

NSSF Statement Request

Request your NSSF contribution statement to track your retirement savings. Our efficient service ensures you receive your detailed NSSF statement showing all contributions and benefits via Email and WhatsApp.

What You Need:

  1. Your NSSF number
  2. National ID copy
  3. Valid email address
  4. Phone number
  5. Period for statement (from-to dates)

Process:

  1. Contact us with your NSSF details and statement period
  2. We verify your identity and account information
  3. Request statement from NSSF portal
  4. Send detailed statement to your email and WhatsApp
Request Statement Now

NSSF Benefits Claims

Claim your NSSF benefits including retirement, disability, survivor, or emigration benefits. Our comprehensive service guides you through the entire claims process and ensures proper documentation.

What You Need:

  1. Your NSSF number and certificate
  2. National ID copy
  3. Bank account details
  4. Medical reports (for disability claims)
  5. Death certificate (for survivor benefits)
  6. Employment termination letter (if applicable)

Process:

  1. Contact us with your claim type and required documents
  2. We review and verify all documentation
  3. Submit benefits claim application to NSSF
  4. Track claim status and provide updates until completion
