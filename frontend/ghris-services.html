GHRIS Services We Offer

Professional assistance with all Government Human Resource Information System services. Our experienced team ensures your GHRIS service requests are handled promptly and efficiently for civil servants and government employees.

GHRIS Registration

Register for Government Human Resource Information System online quickly and easily. Our comprehensive service ensures civil servants and government employees get registered and receive their GHRIS credentials promptly.

What You Need:

  1. National ID or Passport copy
  2. Valid email address and phone number
  3. Employment letter or appointment letter
  4. Payroll number (if available)
  5. Ministry/Department details
  6. Job group and designation information
  7. Bank account details

Process:

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp with your employment details
  2. We verify your government employment status
  3. Submit your registration to GHRIS portal
  4. Create your GHRIS account and credentials
  5. Receive login details and access confirmation

Payslip Access

Access your government payslips through GHRIS portal quickly and easily. Our efficient service ensures you receive your monthly payslips and salary statements via Email and WhatsApp.

What You Need:

  1. Your payroll number
  2. National ID number
  3. Valid email address
  4. Phone number
  5. Ministry/Department information
  6. Employment details

Process:

  1. Contact us with your payroll and employment details
  2. We verify your identity and employment status
  3. Access your payslips from GHRIS portal
  4. Download and format payslips for clarity
  5. Send payslips to your email and WhatsApp immediately

Leave Application

Apply for various types of leave through GHRIS system efficiently. Our service covers annual leave, sick leave, maternity/paternity leave, and other government employee leave applications.

What You Need:

  1. Your payroll number and GHRIS credentials
  2. National ID copy
  3. Leave application details (dates, type, reason)
  4. Medical certificate (for sick leave)
  5. Supporting documents (if required)
  6. Supervisor/HOD contact information

Process:

  1. Contact us with your leave application requirements
  2. We review leave entitlement and eligibility
  3. Submit leave application through GHRIS portal
  4. Track application status and approvals
  5. Receive leave approval confirmation and documentation
Apply for Leave Now

Employee Records Management

Comprehensive management of your government employee records including personal information updates, career progression tracking, and employment history management through GHRIS.

What You Need:

  1. Your payroll number and employee details
  2. National ID copy
  3. Updated personal information
  4. Educational certificates (for updates)
  5. Training certificates and qualifications
  6. Bank account details (for updates)
  7. Next of kin information

Process:

  1. Contact us with your record management needs
  2. We assess current records and required updates
  3. Submit record updates through GHRIS portal
  4. Verify changes with relevant departments
  5. Confirm all updates and provide documentation
