HELB Loan Application

Apply for Higher Education Loans Board funding online quickly and easily. Our comprehensive service covers undergraduate, postgraduate, and technical education loan applications with all required documentation.

What You Need:

  1. National ID or Birth Certificate copy
  2. Valid email address and phone number
  3. University/College admission letter
  4. Fee structure from institution
  5. Parents'/Guardians' ID copies
  6. Family income details and supporting documents
  7. Bank account details

Process:

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp with your application details
  2. We verify all required documents and eligibility
  3. Submit your loan application to HELB portal
  4. Track application status and provide updates
  5. Receive loan approval and disbursement details

HELB Statement Request

Request your HELB loan statement to track your borrowing, disbursements, and repayment history. Our efficient service ensures you receive your detailed HELB statement via Email and WhatsApp.

What You Need:

  1. Your HELB loan number
  2. National ID number
  3. Valid email address
  4. Phone number
  5. Student registration number
  6. Institution details

Process:

  1. Contact us with your HELB details and statement requirements
  2. We verify your identity and loan information
  3. Request statement from HELB portal
  4. Process and format statement for clarity
  5. Send detailed statement to your email and WhatsApp

HELB Clearance Certificate

Obtain your HELB clearance certificate showing your loan repayment status. Essential for employment, further studies, and other official purposes requiring proof of HELB loan clearance.

What You Need:

  1. Your HELB loan number
  2. National ID copy
  3. Valid email address and phone number
  4. Proof of loan repayment (if applicable)
  5. Employment details (for employed applicants)
  6. Purpose for clearance certificate

Process:

  1. Contact us with your clearance certificate requirements
  2. We verify your loan status and repayment history
  3. Submit clearance certificate request to HELB
  4. Process any outstanding issues or payments
  5. Receive official clearance certificate
Get Clearance Certificate Now

HELB Account Management

Comprehensive HELB account management including loan restructuring, repayment plan adjustments, contact information updates, and beneficiary management for your education loan account.

What You Need:

  1. Your HELB loan number and account details
  2. National ID copy
  3. Current employment and income information
  4. Bank account details for repayments
  5. Updated contact information
  6. Supporting documents for account changes

Process:

  1. Contact us with your account management needs
  2. We assess your current loan status and requirements
  3. Submit account update requests to HELB
  4. Negotiate repayment terms if needed
  5. Confirm all account changes and provide documentation
