TSC Services We Offer

Professional assistance with all Teachers Service Commission services. Our experienced team ensures your TSC service requests are handled promptly and efficiently for teachers and education professionals.

TSC Registration

Register with Teachers Service Commission online quickly and easily. Our comprehensive service ensures qualified teachers get registered and receive their TSC number and certification promptly.

What You Need:

  1. National ID or Passport copy
  2. Valid email address and phone number
  3. Teaching qualification certificates
  4. University/College transcripts
  5. Good conduct certificate
  6. Medical certificate
  7. Passport-size photographs
  8. Birth certificate

Process:

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp with your qualification details
  2. We verify your teaching qualifications and documents
  3. Submit your registration to TSC portal
  4. Process application and follow up with TSC
  5. Receive TSC registration number and certificate

Teacher Certification

Obtain your teacher certification and professional licenses through TSC. Our service covers practicing certificates, subject endorsements, and professional development certifications.

What You Need:

  1. Your TSC registration number
  2. National ID copy
  3. Teaching qualification certificates
  4. Employment letter (if employed)
  5. Professional development certificates
  6. Valid email address and phone number

Process:

  1. Contact us with your TSC details and certification needs
  2. We verify your registration and qualification status
  3. Submit certification application to TSC
  4. Process any required assessments or evaluations
  5. Receive teacher certification and practicing license
Get Certified Now

Transfer Applications

Apply for teacher transfers between schools, counties, or regions through TSC system efficiently. Our service covers inter-county transfers, hardship transfers, and administrative transfers.

What You Need:

  1. Your TSC number and employment details
  2. National ID copy
  3. Current posting letter
  4. Reason for transfer (medical, family, etc.)
  5. Supporting documents for transfer reason
  6. Preferred transfer destinations
  7. Head teacher's recommendation

Process:

  1. Contact us with your transfer application requirements
  2. We assess transfer eligibility and documentation
  3. Submit transfer application through TSC portal
  4. Follow up on application status and approvals
  5. Receive transfer approval and new posting details
Apply for Transfer Now

Professional Development

Manage your professional development requirements and career progression through TSC. Our service covers CPD points tracking, career advancement applications, and professional growth documentation.

What You Need:

  1. Your TSC number and current job group
  2. National ID copy
  3. Professional development certificates
  4. Training attendance records
  5. Performance appraisal reports
  6. Additional qualifications (if any)
  7. Career progression application details

Process:

  1. Contact us with your professional development needs
  2. We assess your current status and career goals
  3. Submit professional development applications to TSC
  4. Track CPD points and career progression requirements
  5. Receive career advancement approvals and documentation
