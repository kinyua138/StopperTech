How to Shop with Stopper Tech & Kilimall

1 Create Your Kilimall Account

Visit www.kilimall.co.ke and click "Sign Up" to create your account. Use a valid email address and phone number that you can access easily.

  • Click "Register" on the top right corner
  • Fill in your personal details accurately
  • Verify your email address and phone number
  • Create a strong password for security
2 Set Up Your Address Book

This is the most important step! You must configure your delivery address to our cyber cafe location for pickup.

Use This Delivery Address:

Name: [Your Full Name]

Phone: [Your Phone Number]

Address: Stopper Tech Cyber Cafe

Location: [Your Cyber Cafe Address]

City: [Your City]

Additional Info: "Deliver to Stopper Tech Cyber Cafe for pickup"

Steps to add address:

  • Go to "My Account" → "Address Book"
  • Click "Add New Address"
  • Enter the delivery details exactly as shown above
  • Set this as your default delivery address
  • Save the address
3 Browse and Shop

Browse through thousands of products on Kilimall and add items to your cart. We recommend starting with smaller items for your first order.

  • Use the search function to find specific products
  • Check product reviews and ratings
  • Compare prices from different sellers
  • Add items to your cart
  • Review your cart before checkout
4 Complete Your Order

Proceed to checkout and ensure your delivery address is set to our cyber cafe. Choose your preferred payment method.

  • Review your cart and quantities
  • Confirm delivery address is set to Stopper Tech
  • Choose payment method (M-Pesa, Card, etc.)
  • Apply any discount codes if available
  • Complete the payment process
5 Order Tracking & Communication

After placing your order, keep us informed so we can track the delivery and notify you when it arrives.

Contact Us After Ordering:

WhatsApp: +254 713 159 136

Email: services@stoppertech.co.ke

What to share:

  • Your order number
  • Items ordered
  • Expected delivery date
  • Your contact information
6 Pickup Your Order

We'll notify you immediately when your order arrives at our cyber cafe. Come and collect your items at your convenience.

  • We'll call/text you when the order arrives
  • Bring a valid ID for verification
  • Inspect your items before leaving
  • Keep your receipt for warranty purposes
